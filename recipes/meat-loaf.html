Meat Loaf

Delicious Meat Loaf

Arguably one of the most popular dishes you can make in your own kitchen. Easy to prepare for a weeknight meal. The Maple-Balsamic glaze is a twist on the normal ketchup glaze, and makes a tasty glaze that sweet, spicy and tangy.

Ingredients

  • 1.5# ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 2 T olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 t salt
  • 1/2 c ketchup
  • 2 T balsamic vinegar
  • 1 t oregano
  • 1/4 t pepper
  • bread crumbs
  • 3 T parsley
  • 1 T maple syrup (or honey)
