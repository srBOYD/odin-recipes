Spaghetti

Easy to make Spaghetti reciepes

A must add reciepe for the ones who dont have the time to spend a lot of time cooking but want to make sure the family is fed properly.

Ingredients

  • Angel hair pasta
  • 80/20 ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 20oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1tbsp oregano
  • 2tps of salt
  • 2tps pepper
  • 1/2 bunch parsley chopped
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring 8 cups of water to a boil
  2. Boil pasta for 9-11 minutes
  3. Brown ground beef
  4. Drain grease once beef is browned
  5. Add tomato paste, crushed canned tomatoes and spices
  6. Simmer on low for 45-60 minutes
  7. Combine sauce with noodles then garnish with parmesan and parsley
