Chili Con Carne

Delicious Chili Con Carne with beans

With a little bit of effort Chili Con Carne could be a great dish to make for large gatherings. We will provide you with one of our favorite reciepes. One of the best things about chili is you can add and/or remove ingredients to make your own reciepe, this is why chili cook off's are so popular.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups dried red beans, or 2 (15-ounce) cans red kidney beans or small red beans, drained
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 to 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can tomatoes
  • 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (4.5-ounce) can green chile peppers
  • 2 teaspoons jalapeño pepper, finely chopped, optional
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • Dash cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • Dash ground cloves
  • 1 small bay leaf

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Prepare beans (unless using canned.) Rinse dry beans, cover with cold water to a depth of about 3 inches above beans; let soak overnight or follow soaking directions on package.
  3. Drain beans and transfer to a large saucepan; cover with fresh water.
  4. Cover pan and simmer for about 1 hour or until beans are tender. Drain and place to the side.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet, then brown ground beef, onion, and bell pepper together until ground beef is no longer pink.
  6. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, chile and jalapeño peppers, seasonings, and bay leaf and stir.
  7. Cover and simmer for 1 hour and 30 minutes, adding a little water if needed to thin out. Check and stir frequently.
  8. Add cooked or canned beans and heat through.
  9. Serve in individual bowls with your choice of toppings and enjoy.
